About CCCGJ 关于杰克逊华人基督教会

Founded in 2007 by a group of Chinese immigrant Christians, CCCGJ is a Southern Baptist church rooted in the gospel of Jesus Christ. While our name includes the word “Chinese,” we warmly welcome people of all ethnic backgrounds. At CCCGJ, both English speaking and Mandarin speaking individuals hear the same gospel, grow together in Christ, and serve in God’s kingdom. We offer a bilingual worship service in Mandarin and English every Sunday at 9:30 a.m. In addition, we provide Sunday School classes, fellowship groups, small discipleship groups, and prayer groups in both English and Mandarin.
CCCGJ（杰克逊华人基督教会）由一群华人移民基督徒于2007年创立，是一间扎根于福音的美南浸信会教会。虽然教会的名字中包含“华人”一词，但我们热情欢迎来自各种不同族裔背景的人。在 CCCGJ，讲英文和讲普通话的人都能听到同一个福音，在基督里一同成长，并在神的国度中参与服侍。我们每个主日早上 9:30 提供中英文双语敬拜。同时，教会也设有中英文主日学课程、团契小组、门徒训练小组以及祷告小组，欢迎您的到来和参与。
